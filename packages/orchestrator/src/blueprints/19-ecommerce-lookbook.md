# E-Commerce Immersive Lookbook Blueprint

## Aesthetic Overview
- **Theme**: High-fashion / Minimalist Luxury E-Commerce
- **Color Palette**: `#FAF8F5` warm cream background, `#1A1A1A` charcoal text, `#C5A880` muted gold accents.
- **Typography**: Cormorant Garamond (Italic Serif Headers) & Montserrat (Body).

## Key Components
1. **Horizontal Scroll Gallery**: GSAP-powered horizontal scrolling showcase of high-end product photography.
2. **Magnetic Quick-View Modals**: Liquid-glass floating cards that reveal product specifications on hover.
3. **Interactive Size & Color Selectors**: Micro-animated pill chips for seamless product customization.
