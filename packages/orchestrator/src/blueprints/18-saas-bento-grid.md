# SaaS Bento Grid Pro Blueprint

## Aesthetic Overview
- **Theme**: Ultra-modern clean SaaS
- **Color Palette**: `#0D0E15` dark background, `#6366F1` indigo primary, `#10B981` emerald success, `#F8FAFC` slate text.
- **Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Headers & Body).

## Key Components
1. **Asymmetric Bento Grid**: Staggered cards with varied spans (`md:col-span-2`, `row-span-2`) showcasing product features.
2. **Micro-Chart Visualizations**: Simulated SVG sparklines and activity bars inside bento cards.
3. **Floating Sticky Header**: Pill-shaped navigation bar with dynamic backdrop blur and quick CTA button.
